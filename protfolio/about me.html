Who am I?

About Me:

Born on April 1, 2005, in the bustling city of Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, India, I, Sanchit Agarwal, was radiating promise and ambition.Kerala Samajam Model School was shaping my academic journey under the guidance of the ICSE board. Coming from a family entrenched in business, I was inheriting a legacy of diligence and entrepreneurship. My father, Mr. Deepak Kumar Agarwal, and my mother, Mrs. Mohini Agarwal, were instilling in me values of integrity and perseverance. Alongside, my elder brother, Aditya Agarwal, was serving as my mentor, nurturing my growth. In the realm of education, my intellect was shining. Fueled by curiosity, I was unraveling the world's mysteries and emerging as a scholar of distinction, earning accolades and admiration. Beyond academics, my entrepreneurial spirit was propelling me forward. With astuteness and innovation, I was navigating the business landscape, blending tradition with modernity. I, Sanchit Agarwal, a prodigy from Jamshedpur, was standing poised at the threshold of greatness. My journey was embodying the power of ambition, perseverance, and familial support.

My Hobbies

Whether you're unwinding after a long day, seeking motivation during a workout, or simply exploring different genres and artists, music has the power to evoke emotions and create unforgettable moments. With photography, you capture moments frozen in time, turning fleeting instances into everlasting memories.
